About the Public Complaint Management System

The Public Complaint Management System (PCMS) is an online platform developed to provide citizens with a simple, transparent, and effective mechanism for registering and resolving public grievances.

This portal enables citizens to submit complaints related to public services and government departments from anywhere, at any time, without the need to visit offices physically.

Objective of the Portal

  • To provide a single-window platform for public grievance registration
  • To ensure time-bound redressal of complaints
  • To enhance transparency and accountability in public service delivery
  • To improve communication between citizens and government departments

Key Features

  • Online complaint registration
  • Real-time complaint tracking
  • Department-wise complaint routing
  • Status updates through the portal
  • Secure login for users and officials
  • Protection of personal data and privacy

Who Can Use This Portal?

  • Citizens who wish to report issues related to public services
  • Government officials responsible for complaint resolution
  • Administrators monitoring grievance redressal performance

How the Portal Works

  • The citizen registers a complaint through the portal
  • The complaint is forwarded to the concerned department
  • The department reviews and takes necessary action
  • The status of the complaint is updated on the portal
  • The citizen can track progress and provide feedback

Data Security & Privacy

The portal follows standard security practices to ensure that all personal information and complaint details are kept confidential and accessible only to authorized users.

Commitment to Citizens

The Public Complaint Management System is committed to delivering efficient, transparent, and citizen-centric grievance redressal services, strengthening trust between citizens and the government.
